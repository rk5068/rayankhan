South India

India is a fasinating country because of cultural diversity among its citizens. In the State of Tamil-Nadu the official language of the state (so in both public and private) is Tamil, not Hindi. In the State of Karnataka, the official language is Kannada. South India has a rich tradition in preserving Dravidian culture which was indigious to the subcontinent before the theorized arrival of the Aryans of pre-historic times. North India is heavily influenced by Aryan culture, which is why they speak Hindi and other Sanskrit languages while in South India they speak Dravidian languages like Tamil, Kannada and Malayalam
